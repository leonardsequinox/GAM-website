About us

GAM was established in 2001 in Kitwe and registered with the Zambia Institute of Chartered Accountants (ZICA) in August 2001. Managed by Gibson Aubrey Mutale, the Firm offers professional services to small, medium and large-sized companies. It has a sizeable client base, ranging from educational institutions, manufacturing, farming, micro-finance companies, international supplying and contracting companies and non-governmental organizations (NGOs). The Firm’s head office is strategically located in Kitwe, the central mining town of the Copperbelt Province, which is just about four hours’ drive (45 minutes flight) to Lusaka. GAM opened a branch of the Firm in Lusaka in early 2020 to service clients in the capital and surrounding cities.

Prior to establishing the firm, Gibson worked for 15 years with Deloitte in various capacities including Senior Audit Manager in charge of Ndola office. Gibson also worked for close to 10 years with the Audit Commission in England in various Team Leading roles on both financial audits and grants certification assignments.

Gibson is a Fellow member of both the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (FCCA) and the Zambia Institute of Chartered Accountants (FZICA). Additionally Gibson holds an MSc in Banking and Finance from Sheffield Business School (Sheffield Hallam University) and a BSc (Hons) degree in Mining Engineering from the University of Newcastle upon Tyne, England.

Audit

In accordance with the Companies Act, 2017, a company registered under this Act is required to file with the Registrar of Companies an annual return that includes, inter alia, its audited financial statements. Apart from being a statutory requirement, an independent audit is the foundation for decision-making by most stakeholders. For instance, an independent audit provides assurance to the shareholders that those they have charged with the governance of the company are doing so diligently and applying the resources entrusted to them in an effective and efficient manner. The principal goal of our firm is to provide this assurance. Our audit services are underpinned by a commitment to provide a constructive and challenging audit.

Taxation

We provide Corporation Tax Services that include:-

- Performing Tax computations to include in financial statements. This involves identifying all allowable tax deductible items such as capital allowances and any pre-incorporation expenses.

- Completing and submission of annual returns to the Zambia Revenue Authority (ZRA) by the due deadline date of 21 June following the end of the charge year to which the accounts relate.

- Assisting with completion and submission to ZRA of the provisional income tax return by the due deadline date of 31 March of the charge year to which the accounts relate

- Advise on Pay As You Earn (PAYE) and Value Added Tax (VAT). We complete and submit returns on behalf of clients

Company Secretarial

In accordance with the Companies Act, 2017 of Zambia, a company registered under this Act is required to file with the Registrar of Companies an annual return within 3 months of the earlier of its financial year end and date of the Annual General Meeting (AGM). We provide company secretarial services that cover company incorporation and ensure full compliance with the annual statutory requirements.

Internal Auditing

GAM Chartered Accountants provide management with a diversity of skills to provide assurance on the effectiveness of governance and internal controls in an organisation. It is costly to maintain an in house internal audit function and consequently most organisations opt to outsource this vital function. With our professional staff, we are able to solve all the internal audit needs of an organisation.
